Venue

Photonics Center, Osaka University Suita Campus

All sessions will be held at Photonics Center (P3 building, Osaka University Suita Campus)

Address:
Osaka University Suita Campus, P3 2-1, Yamada-Oka, Suita, Osaka 565-0871, Japan.

Tel:
+81-(0)6-6879-7927

Access

From Kansai International Airport (KIX)

Take airport bus for Osaka Airport (Itami). Every 40min service, 1,900 yen, 70min ride. At Osaka Airport (Itami), ride a taxi (20min, 3000 yen) or a monorail (any). Change train at Banpaku Kinen Koen for "Saito-nishi". Get off at Handai Byoin Mae, which is in front of the campus. Every 10 min service, 30min ride, 420 yen.

From Shin-Osaka Station (Shinkansen)

Take the Subway for Senri-Chuo and get off at Senri-Chuo ( 10 min, 320 yen). At Senri-Chuo, take a taxi (~1500yen) or a bus bound for Handai-hombu-mae or Ibaraki Mihogaoka (210 yen, every 10-20min service, 10min ride), and get off at Handai Hombu Mae which is inside the campus.

From the nearest station or bus stop

15min taxi from "Senri-Chuo Station"

15min walk from "Kita-Senri Station", Hankyu-Senri Line

10min walk from "Handai-byoin-mae", Osaka monorail

5min walk from the bus stop "Handai-Honbu-mae"
