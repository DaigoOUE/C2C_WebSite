Frequently Asked Questions

What should I write Flight No. & Arrival Time if I have not booked flights yet?
Please write your flights you are planning to book. You can make a change later when you find another suitable flight. If there are any changes, please let us know.
Will I get financial supports from the meeting?
We will basically support as following.
Registration: free including foods (lunch and banquet on day 1)
Accommodation: fully supported during November 27th-29th (two nights)
Airline ticket: no support
Do I need to make a reservation for accommodation by myself?
No, you don't. We will make a reservation at Hotel Hankyu Expo Park for your accommodation.
How can I get to Hotel Hankyu Expo Park from Kansai International Airport?
Take Nankai line. At "Namba Station", change trains to Midosuji line. At "Senri-Chuo Station", change trains again to Osaka monorail and get off at "Bannpaku Kinen Koen Station" You can reach the hotel in 2 minutes on foot.
How can I get to the venue (Photonics Center) from Hotel Hankyu Expo Park?
Take Osaka monorail (Saito line) to "Handai-byoin-mae". You can get to the venue in 15 minutes on foot. Refer the page of Venue when it comes to the map from "Handai-byoin-mae" to the venue.

If you have any questions, please contact us by e-mail using the information below.
E-mail : global_student_conference2016@parc.osaka-u.ac.jp
